About

PixxWithAsh is a fashion-focused photography portfolio showcasing runway, backstage, and detail-driven work from New York Fashion Week. The emphasis is on silhouette, fabric, motion, and atmosphere, documenting collections as they unfold in real time.

Each image is composed to reflect the designer’s vision while preserving the raw energy of the show. The work balances editorial clarity with documentary instinct, creating a visual archive of fashion in motion.
